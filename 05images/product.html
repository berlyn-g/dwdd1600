Materials

To make a spoon ring you will need specific material to be able to get the perfect ring that will fit your finger exactly how you want it!

Sterling Spoon and Ring Mandrel

Sterling spoons are the necessity when making a metal ring that will last forever. Antique Sterling spoons are what you need to look for while scavaging through antique stores for spoons. Using this specific metal will make sure your ring does not rust when you wash your hands. It is also much easier to bend when making your ring. A ring mandrel will help you adjust the sizing of the ring so get it to the size you want.

Jeweler's Saw and Ring Bender

A jeweler's saw is used to cut the spoon in half to then make the ring. One of the most important tools when making a spoon ring is a ring bender. This is used to actaully bend and round the spoon into a ring shape. It comes with adjustable sizing to bend it just how you want it.

Metal File and Rubber Mallet

A metal file will help when you finish cutting the spoon in half and need to soften the edges of the metal so it is not scratchy on your finger. Lasly, the rubber mallet can be used to hit the ring into a a more round shap while it is on the ring mandrel.
